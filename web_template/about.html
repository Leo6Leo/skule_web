About Us

As a student-run project directorship overseen by the University of Toronto Engineering Society, our team provides both technical and logistical photography support to our engineering community! Not only do we coordinate photographers to photograph events at Skule™, we can also offer your club/organization photography services such as equipment rental, photo shoot, and free workshops focusing on photography and related skills.
